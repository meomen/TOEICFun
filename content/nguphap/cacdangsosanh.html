2. EQUALITY: ( So Sánh Bằng)

Equality is used to compare two people or things that are equal in quantity or characteristics.

So sánh bằng được sử dụng để so sánh 2 người hay vật mà bằng nhau về số lượng hoặc đặc điểm.

To be or verb (linking)+as+adj+as

Verb (thường) +as+adv+as

Equality ( So Sánh Bằng)

Non- equality( So Sánh Không Bằng)

As+adj/adv+as

Not as or not so+adj/adv+as

You are as crazy my sister

John sings as well as his sister

Mont Balnc is not as high as mount Everest

His job is not so difficult as mine

Note: 1 số trạng từ bổ nghĩa cho so sánh bằng là: nearly, almost, just: gần

Half, twice, three times

Eg: She’s twice as old as him!

The book, the second in series, is nearly as exciting as the first.

3. COMPARATIVE : (So Sánh Hơn)

Comparative is used when two people or things are compared (được dùng khi 2 người hay vật được so sánh).

Short adj/adv+er+than

More +long adj/adv+than

Less +adj/adv+than

- My house is smaller than my friend’s house

- Tokyo is more expensive than new York

- She speaks French more fluently than I do.

- This apple is less flavorful than the other one.

Note: 1 số trạng từ bổ nghĩa cho so sánh hơn là:much, far, a lot (nhiều ) rather, slightly, a bit, a little (hơi)

My car is far more expensive than smith’s

My mother’s hair is slightly longer than mine.

4. SUPERLATIVE (So Sánh Nhất)

The superlative is used when more than two people or things are compared . So sánh nhất được sử dụng khi có nhiều hơn 2 người hoặc vật được so sánh).

The+ short adj/adv+est

The most+long adj/adv

The least+ long adj/adv

- My dog is the biggest of all the dogs in the neiborhood.

- This is the most expensive hotel I’ve ever stayed in.

(so sánh nhất hay đi với thì hiện tại hoàn thành)

- These shoes are the least expensive of all.

5. DOUBLE COMPARISON (So Sánh Kép)

5.1 So Sánh Lũy Tiến : (càng ngày càng)

Adj/adv + er and adj/adv +er

More and more +adj/adv

Less and less + adj/adv

- Please stop making the game easier and easier

- She gets more and more beautiful every time I see her

- Germans less and less satisfied with their jobs

5.2 So Sánh Đồng Tiến : (càng…càng)

The +comparative + Subj +Verb, the +comparative+ Subj+ Verb

- The more work we do, the better grades we will have

- The more you eat, the fatter you will become.

6. OTHERS: Cấu Trúc So Sánh Khác [Học thuộc lòng]

Structures

Examples

No later than (không trễ hơn)

The supervisor expects us to come in to work no later than 7:00

No longer (không còn nữa)

Mary no longer uses make-up

Rather than (hơn là…)

The demonstrator stood his ground rather than run away like the others.

Would rather …than (thích A hơn thích B)

I would rather read a book at home than watch a movie at the theater.
